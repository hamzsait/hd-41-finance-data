About This Data

Schedule A campaign finance records are sourced from the Texas Ethics Commission (ethics.state.tx.us). Donors are resolved to person-level identities by name + ZIP + employer signals across the unified pool, so the same person giving to multiple candidates counts once. Federal partisan lean is computed from FEC schedule A contributions; Texas state-level lean comes from Texas Ethics Commission bulk filings. Affiliation flags — AIPAC, ADL, Israel-aligned giving, oil & gas, real estate, military industrial complex — require at least one evidence row per finding. Findings marked “sensitive” are flagged for human review before publication.
